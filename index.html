About Design Systems

What are they and why do they matter?

What are Design Systems? Why should I use one? What if I don't?
They contain items like: UI Kits, Interaction Designs, Usability Patterns, Development Components, Language Definition, and more! They help your team to speak the same language and also iterate much faster through both prototypes and live software. You'll continue to feel the pain of technical debt, change management, poor ux, high customer support requests, and lengthy times to develop and launch new software.
